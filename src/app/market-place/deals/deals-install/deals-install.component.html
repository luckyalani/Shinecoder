The CedCommerce Integration is a powerful solution that seamlessly connects and synchronizes your Zoho Inventory and order management processes. It helps you streamline business operations by syncing Inventory with multiple channels, such as Magento, WooCommerce, and BigCommerce. With real-time data synchronization, this integration empowers businesses to optimize inventory control, enhance productivity, and deliver a superior customer experience.

key Features:

  • Order management systems (OMS) provide automation and integration across every step of the order journey. Receive orders from any ecommerce platform and fulfill them from a centralized Zoho Inventory account.
  • The app automates inventory sync between your Zoho Inventory account and connected channel(s). Its seamless integration with Magento, WooCommerce, BigCommerce, and others significantly simplify your business operations.
  • You can manage multiple stores inventories and orders from a single location. API communications allow information to flow seamlessly between Zoho Inventory accounts and connected ecommerce stores. This enables sellers to manage and track everything centrally without switching between platforms.
  • The integration provides near real-time updates between your Zoho Inventory account and connected platforms. This way, you only need to track inventory in the Zoho account, and the app will take care of the rest.
